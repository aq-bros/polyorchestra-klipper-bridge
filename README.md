# PolyOrchestra™ Klipper Bridge 

![License: AGPL v3](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-AGPL_v3-blue.svg)
![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/Python-3.x-yellow.svg)
![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/Platform-Klipper%20%2F%20Raspberry%20Pi-green.svg)

The official, open-source bridge to connect Klipper 3D printers to the **PolyOrchestra™** ecosystem.

This lightweight service runs alongside Moonraker, securely transmitting telemetry, print status, and history to your **PolyOrchestra Dashboard** (available on **macOS, Windows, iOS, and Android**). It allows you to monitor and manage your fleet from any device—desktop, tablet, or mobile—without exposing your printer to the internet.

---

## Getting Started

The installation and configuration process is fully guided through the **PolyOrchestra™ App**.

1. Download the app for your device.
2. Go to **"Add Printer"**.
3. Follow the on-screen interactive tutorial for your specific setup.
